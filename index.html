Over the years, there have been many rumors as to why the Russian never fought under the UFC banner, with him previously stating that one of the reasons is because he felt that Dana White hated him. They wanted part of the promotion, they wanted to own a piece of everything, they wanted a lot. “Because they knew that with Fedor, they had the golden ticket, and they wanted to play it out as much as possible.
